Dr. (Mrs.) Surheeta Kareem

Dr. (Mrs.) Surheeta Kareem ( Director )
M.B.B.S,M.S.F.I.C.M.C.H.,
F.I.A.G.E.S Laparoscopy & infertility

Dr. Surheeta kareem is a renowned Senior gynecologist of Gorakhpur. Dr.(Mrs) Surheeta Kareem is an expert laparoscopic Surgeon and Director of S.F.A.L. She has been trained in laparoscopy and hysteroscopy from Germany. She also took training in A.R.T. technique at Mumbai . Dr.(Mrs) Surheeta Kareem has completed her MBBS from reputed colleges and University.She has done fellowship course such as F.I.C.M.C.H and F.I.A.G.E.S. She specialises in Laparoscopic Surgery and Infertility.

She Started S.A.F.A.L. ( Star Advanced Fertilty and Laparoscopy ) IVF center in 2005.Safal is the first test tube center in eastern U.P.

Three decades ago when we started the project of Star Hospital Pvt. Ltd., we had a distinct vision of providing Eastern U. P. and basically Gorakhpur ample medical facilities under one roof which the people of this part had not seen before. Though some oddities stood in front of us but with the grace of God and a sense of excitement and responsibility, we succeed in amplifying our chances of realizing our vision. Star Hospital came up as the first multi specialty medical center in an area which only had the local district hospital and a medical college hospital for catering to a population of more than 15, Lacs in and around Gorakhpur. As we move sure-footed towards becoming a center of medical excellence, we smell the compelling call of success.We are the first in this region to introduce the concept of laparoscopic surgery as a diagnostic tool way back in the year 1991 and to establish a diagnostic center fully equipped with all the major equipments. All the required facilities are available in the same hospital premises. At Star Hospital, over a dozen acclaimed physician and surgeon together with more than 135 staff provide a vibrant and cordial atmosphere for the patients. It is our conviction that patients’ need are more completely served when there is a continuous interaction among patient care, education and research Star Hospital remains in the forefront of medical excellence of international significance. We believe that in the coming decade, we will be multi specialty organization emboldened by success and seasoned by our journey from the developing arena to an international scenario, we are setting scores for our next symphony. Medical excellence will give impetus to future growth, while dedications and advancement would keep the rhythm. Even as we savor the performance of recent years, excitement and anticipation punctuate our preparation for the future-identifying new competencies we need to build and gaps we need to bridge, to ensure another winning score.
